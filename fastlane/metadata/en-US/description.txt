RunRun helps you keep track of your running journey.

It automatically syncs your runs from Apple Watch, Strava, Nike Run Club, and other apps via Apple Health. Review your progress on a simple, clean interface and share it easily.

Completely free with no annoying ads.

【Features】
• View monthly and yearly distance with graphs
• Share to Instagram and other social media easily
• See your running days at a glance on the calendar
• Visualize routes with pace-based gradient colors
• Track daily progress with home screen widgets
• Share activities with friends on the timeline
• Compete with friends on monthly rankings

【Compatible Apps】
• Apple Watch Workouts
• Strava
• Nike Run Club
• Garmin Connect
• Other apps that sync with Apple Health

【Perfect For】
• Building a running habit
• Reviewing your monthly and yearly progress
• Keeping your favorite recording app
