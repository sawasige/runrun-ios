RunRun connects with your favorite running apps to share and compete on monthly records with friends.

Your runs from Apple Watch, Strava, Nike Run Club, and other apps that sync with Apple Health are automatically imported.

【Features】
• View monthly running records
• Compete with friends on monthly distance rankings
• Check friends' activities on the timeline
• Visualize routes with pace-based gradient colors
• Track monthly and yearly progress with cumulative distance graphs
• Home screen widgets (Calendar & Distance Progress)

【Compatible Apps】
• Apple Watch Workouts
• Strava
• Nike Run Club
• Other apps that sync with Apple Health

【Perfect For】
• Building a running habit
• Staying motivated with friends
• Tracking your monthly progress

Add friends and discover the joy of running together.
