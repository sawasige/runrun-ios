RunRun is not a recording app.

Just run with your favorite app—Apple Watch, Strava, Nike Run Club, Garmin, and more. Your runs sync automatically via Apple Health, and RunRun visualizes them beautifully.

【Features】
• View monthly and yearly distance with graphs
• See your running days at a glance on the calendar
• Visualize routes with pace-based gradient colors
• Track daily progress with home screen widgets
• Share activities with friends on the timeline

【Compatible Apps】
• Apple Watch Workouts
• Strava
• Nike Run Club
• Garmin Connect
• Other apps that sync with Apple Health

【Perfect For】
• Building a running habit
• Reviewing your monthly and yearly progress
• Keeping your favorite recording app

Run with your favorite app. Review with RunRun.
