FAQ

My running records aren't showing

Please check the following:

  • Have you granted RunRun access to your health data? (Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → RunRun)
  • Are you recording runs with Apple Watch, Strava, Nike Run Club, or other compatible apps?
  • Try pulling down to refresh the app

I'm not showing up in the rankings

To appear in rankings, your data needs to be synced. Try the "Re-sync" button in Settings.

I want to delete my account

You can delete your account from "Settings" → "Delete Account" in the app. Deleting your account will permanently remove all data stored on our servers.
